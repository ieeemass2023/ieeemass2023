Final Paper Submission

Final Version Submission

The camera-ready version of accepted papers should be submitted by July 14, 2023 (for all main conference papers, poster and demo papers and workshop papers). Note that the camera-ready paper and copyright submissions are NOT through EDAS. Please follow the instructions below to submit the camera-ready version of your paper and a copyright form at the following website: https://ieeecps.org/#!/auth/login?ak=1&pid=6bEvcCCTIbZPudtoZDepZa.

Authors need to create an account first. The first page will be a "Sign In" page. After signing in, please follow the step-by-step instructions. Authors will also submit copyright forms during the process. Please DO NOT submit your camera-ready paper to EDAS.

For regular main conference papers, the page limit for the camera-ready paper is 9 pages. For short main conference papers, the page limit for the camera-ready paper is 7 pages. No extra page is allowed for conference papers. Poster and demo papers should be 2 pages. Workshop papers' page-limit is 6 pages.

All papers must follow the standard IEEE camera-ready format as specified for the initial submission in Call for Papers.
