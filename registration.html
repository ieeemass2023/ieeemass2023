Registration Policy

  • Author registration deadline for conference/workshop is July 24, 2023.
  • At least one author of an accepted conference/workshop paper must register at the conference non-student rate by July 24, 2023. An author with multiple accepted conference paper can have the discounted rate for any additional paper.
  • Member registration rates are available for professional members of IEEE.
  • Conference paper page limit is 9 (short papers are 7 pages and posters are 2 pages). Workshop paper page limit is 6.
  • Registration can be canceled on or before September 1. 2023. There will be an administrative fee of $100 deducted from each refund.
  • For registration questions, please email shuhuiyang@pnw.edu.
